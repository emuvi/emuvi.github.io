Experience
Early Years

I started developing systems at age 14 in 1998 with Delphi. The RAD way of frame user interfaces captivated my young mind. I've been fascinated with computers some time already but then I realized that was infinitely more interesting to create on it. know more...

A miraculous thing happen with me at this age. I attended a course on Object Oriented Programming with Delphi in the Senai/CTAI. Half of this course was on Delphi syntax and half was about OO semantics. So, very early, I was presented to inheritance, encapsulation and polymorphism. I must confess that on the time the syntax draw much more of my attention. So young I could not realize yet that is the semantics of a source code that makes it god or bad. I like to remember also of this early years my first commercial project, a very small system to a restaurant to automatize and register some calculations they had to keep track.
